# User Guide - Overview

## Purpose

Dove365 Support helps support teams manage customer support from intake to resolution. It combines customer records, managed service allocation, case handling, queues, support activities, dashboards, and automation in one model-driven app.

## Navigation

Typical app areas include:

- Overview and dashboards
- Customers: Accounts and Contacts
- Service Delivery: Managed Services and Service Offerings
- Support: Cases, Queues, Queue Items, Categories, Subcategories, and Support Activities

Exact navigation can vary by security role.

## Core records

- Account: the customer organisation.
- Contact: the person at the customer.
- Service Offering: the service type selected on Cases and Managed Services.
- Managed Service: the support allocation or agreement.
- Case: the support request.
- Support Activity: time and activity logged against a Case.
- Queue: a work container.
- Queue Item: a Case placed into a Queue.
- Category and Subcategory: Case classification.

## End-to-end lifecycle

1. An Account and Contact exist or are created.
2. A Managed Service may be created for the Account.
3. A Case is created manually or from the support mailbox.
4. The Case is assigned a Category, Subcategory, Service Offering, and Managed Service where applicable.
5. The Case is added to a Queue.
6. A user picks the Queue Item or begins working it.
7. The user logs Support Activities and time.
8. Managed Service hours are updated.
9. The Case is resolved and the customer is notified.

## Best practice

- Keep Account and Contact data accurate.
- Always classify Cases.
- Pick Queue Items before working them.
- Log Support Activities promptly.
- Review Managed Service usage before committing additional work.
- Resolve Cases only when the customer issue is complete.
