Documentation / CRM Overview

What is Dove365 CRM — Starter?

A complete CRM built on Microsoft Power Platform and Dataverse — designed for small and medium businesses that need structure, not complexity.

Why CRM Matters

Stop managing customers in spreadsheets

Without a CRM, customer relationships live in email inboxes, spreadsheets, and individual heads. Data gets lost, follow-ups get missed, and no one has a complete picture.

01

No single source of truth

Customer data is scattered across multiple tools, people, and formats. There’s no single record that shows the full picture of a customer relationship — who spoke to them, what was agreed, what’s outstanding.

02

Leads fall through the gaps

Prospects are tracked manually or not at all. Follow-up relies on memory. Qualified leads get lost because no one has visibility of where they are in the process or who is responsible.

03

Support suffers without structure

Support requests arrive via email, phone, and chat. Without a structured system, issues get forgotten, priorities get confused, and customers feel they need to chase for updates.

The Solution

A structured CRM inside Microsoft 365

Dove365 CRM — Starter is a fully configured, productised CRM built on Microsoft Power Platform. It runs inside Dataverse — Microsoft’s enterprise data platform — and integrates with Outlook and SharePoint.

✓

Built on Microsoft Dataverse

Microsoft’s relational data platform, powering millions of business apps worldwide. Secure, scalable, and AI-ready from day one.

✓

Outlook Integration

Track emails against CRM records without leaving Outlook. Server-side sync keeps your activity history current automatically.

✓

SharePoint Integration

Store and access documents against customer records. Files live in SharePoint, linked directly to accounts, contacts, and opportunities.

✓

Business Process Flows

Step-by-step guided processes for leads, opportunities, and cases ensure your team follows consistent, repeatable workflows.

✓

Dashboards and Reporting

Built-in dashboards give management visibility of pipeline, cases, and activity at a glance — no separate reporting tool required.

✓

Built-in Training and Support

Training material and a support framework are included in every deployment. Your team knows how to use the system from day one.

Ideal Customers

Designed for businesses that are growing

Dove365 CRM — Starter is built for small and medium businesses using Microsoft 365 that need to bring structure to their customer relationships without enterprise complexity or enterprise cost.

01

Professional services firms

Consultancies, agencies, accountancies, and legal firms managing client relationships, project pipelines, and recurring work across a team that needs consistent visibility.

02

B2B sales teams

Businesses with a defined sales process that need visibility of leads, pipeline stage, deal value, and win and loss outcomes without the overhead of a full enterprise CRM implementation.

03

Customer-facing teams

Any team that handles support requests, issues, onboarding, or service enquiries and needs a structured way to track, assign, prioritise, and resolve them consistently.

Product Modules

Everything in one system

Dove365 CRM — Starter is organised into four areas covering your full customer lifecycle — from first contact through to ongoing support and relationship management.

1

Relationship

Accounts and Contacts. Your core customer database — every organisation and person you work with, linked together in a structured record.

2

Pipeline

Leads and Opportunities. Manage early-stage prospects and track qualified deals through your sales process from first conversation to close.

3

Service

Cases and Service Offerings. Handle support requests and service enquiries. Categorise work consistently using a shared service catalogue.

4

Activity

Emails, Tasks, Calls, Appointments. A complete communication history against every record. Always know what happened and what’s next.
