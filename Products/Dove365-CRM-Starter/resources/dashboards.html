CRM & My Work Dashboards

Use CRM-wide context and your personal workload view to decide what needs attention in Dove365 CRM — Starter.

My Work

Your work, at a glance

My Work is filtered to the logged-in user. It combines owned CRM records with open activities so each person can begin the day with the work that needs action.

KPIWhat it means
My LeadsActive leads owned by you.
My OpportunitiesActive opportunities owned by you.
My CasesActive cases owned by you.
Upcoming TasksOpen owned activities with a due date that has not passed.
Overdue TasksOpen owned activities whose due date has passed.

Troubleshooting

If a dashboard looks empty

No owned recordsConfirm the logged-in user owns active Leads, Opportunities, or Cases.
Activities are missingConfirm activities are open, owned by the user, and have due dates.
Rows do not openOpen the dashboard inside Dynamics and confirm permission to read the target record.
Request errorCapture the displayed diagnostics and ask an administrator to check Dataverse permissions and web-resource context.
