Managed Service Automation

Understand the automation that keeps managed service usage, thresholds, resets, and support activity rollups current.

Purpose

Usage tracking for support allocations

Managed Service automation helps account managers and support teams understand consumed hours, remaining allocation, usage percentage, and when attention is required.

Usage Threshold

Daily Managed Service 80% Usage Threshold

This automation identifies Managed Services that are at or above 80 percent usage so teams can review support coverage before an allocation is exhausted.

  • 80 percent or above means the allocation is approaching its limit.
  • 100 percent means the allocation is fully used.
  • More than 100 percent means the customer is over allocation.

Admin checks

Managed Services have allocation values
Support Activities are logged accurately
Account Managers know how to review alerts
Allocation Reset

Monthly allocation reset

The monthly reset automation clears or resets usage values for Managed Services in line with the organisation’s support agreement model.

✓

Confirm the reset schedule

The reset should align with the commercial support period agreed with customers.

✓

Check reporting expectations

Before changing the schedule, confirm what account managers expect to see in monthly reporting.

✓

Validate after reset

After the reset runs, confirm current allocation, used hours, remaining hours, and usage percentage values are correct.

Hours Rollup

Aggregate Support Activity hours

Support Activities are the time-based records used to record work performed against a Case. The aggregation process keeps Managed Service usage aligned with logged work.

  1. User logs a Support Activity against a Case.
  2. The Case is linked to the relevant Managed Service where applicable.
  3. The aggregation process updates used hours and remaining allocation.
  4. Dashboards and account review information reflect the latest usage position.
Incorrect or missing Support Activities lead to inaccurate managed service usage. Train users to log time consistently.
